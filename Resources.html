Books

Some books about designing or related to designing.

Design Fundamentals & Theory

  • Design for the Real World: Human Ecology and Social Change (Viktor Papanek) Goodreads
  • The Design of Everyday Things (Don Norman) Goodreads
  • Grid Systems in Graphic Design (Josef Muller-Brockmann) Goodreads
  • Design and Form: The Basic Course at the Bauhaus and Later (Johannes Itten) Goodreads
  • Design: History, Theory and Practice of Product Design (Bernhard E. Burdek) Documen Pub, Amazon
  • Change by Design (Tim Brown) IDEO
  • About Face: The Essentials of Interaction Design (Alan Cooper et al.) Amazon

UX, User Experience & Research

  • Contextual Design: Defining Customer-Centred System (Hugh Beyer, Karen Holtzblatt) ACM Digital Library
  • Experience Design: Technology for All the Right Reasons (Marc Hassenzahl) Research Gate
  • Hidden in Plain Sight (Jan Chipchase) Research Gate
  • Sketching User Experiences: Getting the Design Right and the Right Design (Bill Buxton) Amazon
  • Sketching User Experiences: The Workbook (Saul Greenberg et al.) Science Direct

Media, Games & Innovation

  • Theory of Fun for Game Design (Raph Koster) Goodreads
  • Designing Interactions (Bill Moggridge) Amazon, Mit Press
  • Designing Media (Bill Moggridge) Mit Press
  • Ecosystem of Innovation (Barry Katz) UCU YouTube, Medium, Nodo
  • Watches Tell More Than Time (Del Coates) Archive
  • By Design: Why There Are No Locks on the Bathroom Doors in the Hotel Louis XIV and Other Object Lessons (Ralph Caplan) Goodreads
  • Paradox of Choice (Barry Schwartz) Wiki, Ted

  • Bubble — The visual control you know and love, now supercharged by AI. Bubble turns ideas into fully scalable apps — fast.

  • Relume.io — Websites designed & built with AI. Sitemaps, Wireframes and Styleguides.
  • Trae — Trae IDE integrates seamlessly into your workflow, collaborating with you to maximize performance and efficiency.
  • Zencoder — The most integrated, customizable, and intuitive coding agent.

Mockups

  • Maneken — The browser powered mockup editor.
  • Rotato — Create stunning 3D device mockups and animations for your app or website designs.

Visual Effects

  • Endless Tools — Meet the all-in-one web tool for quickly crafting striking 3D visuals, animations, and unique effects.

Drawing

  • Krita — A professional FREE and open source painting program. It is made by artists that want to see affordable art tools for everyone.

Accessibility

  • Microsoft Inclusive Design — A methodology, born out of digital environments, that enables and draws on the full range of human diversity.

Management

  • Web Design Project Management Kanban — Project management board to facilitate collaboration and discussion between my team and yours throughout the consulting and developing process.

Portfolios and Inspiration

  • Wiby.me — Search engine is building a web of pages as it was in the earlier days of the internet. In addition, Wiby helps vintage computers to continue browsing the web, as pages indexed are more suitable for their performance.

Typography

A curated collection of my favorite typefaces, font foundries, and typography tools.

Typefaces

  • Road UI — a new Ukrainian typeface by Andrii Konstantynov, version of the Road UA traffic font. Highly legible and versatile, now adapted for interfaces.
  • Hvntr Free — favorite free Y2K-style fonts.
  • Editorial New — a serif font from the legendary Pangram Pangram.
  • Departure Mono — a contemporary monospaced font with a lo-fi vibe.
  • Kyiv Metro Fonts — Kyiv metro font collection, free for Ukrainians, donation-based for others.
  • Geist Sans — A typeface specifically designed for developers and designers. We began by creating a monospace version that prioritized readability and seamlessly integrated into coding environments.

Type Foundries & Libraries

  • Fontshare — free font collection.
  • ABCDinamo — independent type foundry with cutting-edge fonts.
  • 205TF — French independent type foundry.
  • Kyiv Type Foundry — Ukrainian type design studio.
  • Open Foundry — open-source and free fonts.
  • Pangram Pangram — global type foundry, contemporary fonts.
  • The League of Moveable Type — pioneering open-source font community.
  • Klim Type Foundry — Klim Type Foundry (Klim) was founded by Kris Sowersby in 2005 and is based in Te Whanganui-a-Tara/Wellington, Aotearoa/New Zealand.
  • Featured Type — A curated showcase of exceptional typefaces, both classic and contemporary.
  • Grid Type — An experimental type tool. You can generate different types of grids with it, draw your letters within these grids and download them as a font file.

Etcetera

  • px to rem Converter — handy tool for converting px to rem.
  • Atypography Manual — Atypography is an art movement that graphically represents traditional writing systems in an unconventional way, creating an authentic design that remains readable while concealing text signs at first glance.

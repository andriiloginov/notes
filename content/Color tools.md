There are loads of cool tools for designers on the web, and we all have our go-to tools for working with color. Here are my favs.

## Palette Generators
- [Coolors](https://coolors.co/) — Create, save, and collect color palettes.
- [Color Lisa](https://colorlisa.com/) — Color palettes of famous artists.
- [Khroma](https://www.khroma.co/) — AI-powered personalized palette generator.
- [Learn UI](https://www.learnui.design/tools/data-color-picker.html) — Palettes for charts, maps, and diagrams.
- [Happy Hues](https://www.happyhues.co/) — Palette inspiration with real-world usage examples.

## Color Tools & Systems
- [Tailwind CSS Color Generator](https://uicolors.app/) — Generate custom color scales.
- [Material Design](https://m2.material.io/design/color/the-color-system.html#tools-for-picking-colors) — UI color tool from Google.
- [Color Box](https://colorbox.io/) — Open-source color system.

## Inspiration & Fun Palettes
- [Colors LOL](https://colors.lol/) — Palettes with fun, descriptive names.
- [Mymind](https://access.mymind.com/colors) — Unique color combinations for your projects.

## Accessibility & Contrast
- [Randoma11ly](https://randoma11y.com/) — Random accessible color combinations.
- [Geenes](https://geenes.app/) — Detailed contrast checker.
- [Stripe's Accessible Systems](https://stripe.com/blog/accessible-color-systems) — On color contrast and accessibility.

## Gradients
- [CSS Hero](https://www.csshero.org/mesher/) — Mesh gradient generator with CSS.
- [Mesh Gradient](https://meshgradient.in/) — Mesh gradients based on palettes.

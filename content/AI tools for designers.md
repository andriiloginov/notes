AI will not replace designers; designers will be replaced by those who use AI. Here's a list of tools to help you generate, research, and automate the process.

## Research & Ideation
-  [The AI Toolbox for Innovators](https://ai.boardofinnovation.com/) 
    Test, pivot or generate ideas in minutes with our free AI tools.
- [User Persona](https://userpersona.dev/) 
    Create FREE User Personas for Your Product or Service
- [Value Proposition Canvas](https://valueprop.dev/) 
    Use our AI-powered tool to create a Value Proposition Canvas for your company based on its description
-  [Predict Human Behavior](https://www.neuronsinc.com/predict) 
     AI copilot built on neuroscience
- [Attention Insight](https://attentioninsight.com/) 
    See how consumers engage with your designs before the launch

## Design & Prototyping
- [Leonardo AI](https://leonardo.ai/) 
    Leverage generative AI with a unique suite of tools to convey your ideas to the world
- [Typper](https://typper.io/) 
    Generate and edit images, texts and functionally codes with the power of Figma and AI
- [UIzard](https://uizard.io/) 
    Turn product ideas into concepts instantly with GenAI
- [Galileo AI](https://www.usegalileo.ai/explore) 
    Generate interface designs at lightning speed
- [Taskade](https://www.taskade.com/templates/design) 
    AI Design Templates
- [Design Tokens Name Generator](https://namedesigntokens.guide/) 
    Create consistent and scalable design token naming conventions for your design system

- [Img Upscaler](https://imgupscaler.com/)

- [Hyperhuman Rodin](https://hyperhuman.deemos.com/)
    Generate 3D assets
- [Nightmare](https://www.nightmare-ai.com/)

- [Pixelcut](https://www.pixelcut.ai/)

AI will not replace designers; designers will be replaced by those who use AI. Here's a list of tools to help you generate, research, and automate the process.

## Research & Ideation
1. [The AI Toolbox for Innovators](https://ai.boardofinnovation.com/) 
    Test, pivot or generate ideas in minutes with our free AI tools.
2. [User Persona](https://userpersona.dev/) 
    Create FREE User Personas for Your Product or Service
3. [Value Proposition Canvas](https://valueprop.dev/) 
    Use our AI-powered tool to create a Value Proposition Canvas for your company based on its description
4. [Predict Human Behavior](https://www.neuronsinc.com/predict) 
     AI copilot built on neuroscience
5. [Attention Insight](https://attentioninsight.com/) 
    See how consumers engage with your designs before the launch

## Design & Prototyping
6. [Leonardo AI](https://leonardo.ai/) 
    Leverage generative AI with a unique suite of tools to convey your ideas to the world
7. [Typper](https://typper.io/) 
    Generate and edit images, texts and functionally codes with the power of Figma and AI
8. [UIzard](https://uizard.io/) 
    Turn product ideas into concepts instantly with GenAI
9. [Galileo AI](https://www.usegalileo.ai/explore) 
    Generate interface designs at lightning speed
10. [Taskade](https://www.taskade.com/templates/design) 
     AI Design Templates

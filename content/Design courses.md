No profession can be mastered in a few months. It's better to make learning a habit. Here's a useful list for designers at all levels, most of which are free.

## Foundations of Design & Graphic Design
1. Graphic Design Specialization, [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/graphic-design )
2. The Language of Design: Form and Meaning, [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/learn/design-language)
3. Intro to Design Thinking, [EdEx](https://www.edx.org/learn/design-thinking/edx-try-it-intro-to-design-thinking)
4. Web Design, [Code Academy](https://www.codecademy.com/catalog/subject/web-design)

## UX/UI & User Research
5. Google UX Design Professional Certificate, [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/google-ux-design)
6. User Experience: The Beginner's Guide, [IxDF](https://www.interaction-design.org/courses/user-experience-the-beginner-s-guide?r=andrii-loginov)
7. Introduction to User Experience Principles and Processes, [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/learn/introtoux-principles-and-processes)
8. UI Design Patterns for Successful Software, [IxDF](https://www.interaction-design.org/courses/ui-design-patterns-for-successful-software?r=andrii-loginov)
9. User Research: Methods and Best Practices, [IxDF](https://www.interaction-design.org/courses/user-research-methods-and-best-practices?r=andrii-loginov)
10. Learn UX writing and content design, [UX Hub](https://course.uxwritinghub.com/free_course)

## Animation, 3D & Game Design
11. Basic 3D Animation using Blender, [EdEx](https://www.edx.org/learn/animation/iitbombay-basic-3d-animation-using-blender)
12. 3D Design with Spline, [Class Central](https://www.classcentral.com/classroom/youtube-3d-design-with-spline-134058)
13. Introduction to Game Design, [EdEx](https://www.edx.org/learn/game-design/massachusetts-institute-of-technology-introduction-to-game-design)
14. TouchDesigner Beginner Course, [YouTube](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLFrhecWXVn5862cxJgysq9PYSjLdfNiHz&si=XECV6t9Sd1t0KDBt)

## Technology, AI & Modern Tools
15. AI for Designers, [IxDF](https://www.interaction-design.org/members/andrii-loginov/certificate/course/b8d01df5-afa7-48c2-b5d7-602acb221a57?certificateType=course)
16. Generative AI Explained, [Nvidia](https://learn.nvidia.com/courses/course-detail?course_id=course-v1:DLI+S-FX-07+V1)
17. Webflow 101, [Webflow](https://university.webflow.com/courses/webflow-101)

19. TechBridge: English for IT 1, [Notecad](https://www.netacad.com/courses/english-for-it1?courseLang=en-US&instance_id=5573e530-8365-4685-baa7-ce1505ed1d2e)
